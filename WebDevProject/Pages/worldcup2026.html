The 2026 FIFA World Cup will be hosted by the United States, Mexico, and Canada.

Host Countries

United States, Mexico, and Canada

The 2026 FIFA World Cup will be the first World Cup hosted by three countries. It will also be the first men's World Cup with 48 teams.

2026 Tournament Format & Groups

  • 48 national teams
  • 104 total matches
  • 16 host cities
  • Three host countries: USA, Mexico, and Canada
  • The tournament will include a Round of 32 before the Round of 16

The 2026 FIFA World Cup will feature 48 teams divided into 12 groups. The top teams from each group will advance to the knockout stage.
